Virtual Teaching

The onset of technology has brought a tremendous transformation in the teaching and learning process. Today, teaching activities are carried out online on various platforms whereby teachers and students are physically separated during classes but then connected through the internet. To accommodate the needs of students and teachers for the virtual classes, we design an interactive online learning platform on Ohyay to make classes more engaging and fun. In addition, we also propose a platform to upload materials and assignments to make everything easier for everyone.

Description

To accommodate the need for an interactive online learning platform, we design two platforms for students and teachers to make online classes more engaging and fun. Our goal is to make it flexible, fun, interactive and convenient for every user.

OHYAY DESCRIPTION

Ohyay is a virtual event platform that allows anyone to create their own real-time, interactive virtual experiences. Through Ohyay, teachers can conduct classes virtually and communicate with learners in a more interactive manner. The platform is completely free and will be a great tool for the institution.

GOOLE CLASSROOM DESCRIPTION

Google Classroom is your all-in-one place for teaching and learning. The platform is easy to use and has tools that helps educators manage, measure, and enrich learning experiences. Teachers can create classrooms for every subject and issue out assignments to leaners as well as save study materials on google drive via google classroom.
